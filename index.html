Projects

Senior Design Project

My senior design project at SDSU was a 4P1S, lithium-ion-phosphate, battery management system (BMS). Our system monitored cell voltages, total pack voltages, temperature, and input/output current to stay within our designated range. It could output up to 15 amps of current and had a text display to show the user charge percentage, error codes, and other data. Personally, I designed the entire codebase in C to assure all data points gathered through I2C were in our designated range, used serial communication to send data to the text display, and set up hardware interrupts to watch the temperature and output current for dangerous levels.
